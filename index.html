Frequently asked questions
 
How do I add Buggy
to my discord server?

You can simply click the button at the top left of this website,
click on "Add to server" and follow the simple installation process provided by discord.
Do not forget to provide BuggyBot with administrator permissions,
otherwise he will unfortunately not work correctly.

 
The bot is currently shown offline
on discord,
what do I do?

Unfortunately we do not have the ressources to keep Buggy online at all times.
If he is offline, you'll have to wait until he is back online.
We look forward to changing this in the near future.

 
It would be nice if the bot
would have another feature,
will it be there soon?

We are constantly working to keep Buggy up to date and free of bugs.
We are also consistently adding new features and if you have a good idea,
you are welcome to send one of the members of the project a message.
